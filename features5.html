POSITION OF RESPONSIBILITIES

About P.O.R

St. Paul's School - an ICSE medium school. The School has been doing this for years to make the students realise and understand how to handle a responsibility. The school has four houses and in each houses, 4 House captains are made consisting of boys and girls. These house captains are made incharge of major works during many events throughout the year. Aprt from the captains, the school also appoints a Head Boy & a Head Girl. These two positions are most important as they are indirectly the leaders of all other positions below them. Apart from this during events, separate event management team is also build, who contributes in the managemnet of the event. These positions of responsibilities are very important, as one who holds it is able to learn a number of skills and things which stays with them throughout their their whole life.
