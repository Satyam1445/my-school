About School
St. Paul's School

St. Paul's School - an ICSE medium school is situated in the heart of the city Begusarai. The School hosts to more than 3000 students from classes Nursery to all the way up to XII. The school also has number of versatile and experienced teachers who excels in teaching any subject in any class at any time. Students from the school has also participated in many national and regional competitions and made the school proud. The school celebrates it's birthday on 2nd August every year. Each year the school has annual sports day, Christmas day, Children's day and many more.
