FEATURE 4

EXTRA CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES
About Activities

St. Paul's School - an ICSE medium school also makes sure that the students take part in various activities as much as possible to find and hone their skills, hobbies. School motivates students to take part in various olympiads, other states competitions, where many students have excelled. Apart from educational events, The school also conducts events on Christmas Day, Annual Sports Day, children's day, republic day, independence day, teachers day, etc. On Teachers Day, students from class 10 and above are given opportunity to become teacher for one day and teach lower class students. This is done in order to make them understand how to handle a room full of audience.
