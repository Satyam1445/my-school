SPORTS FACILITIES

About Sports Facilities

St. Paul's School - an ICSE medium school also has a central ground completely dedicated to various sports that are held throughout the year for different classes of students. Sports and fitness is an important part of student's life so, it is been taken care off. The school is host to various sports like Football, Badminton, Atheletics, Kho-Kho, etc. Students from different houses of school i.e. RED,GREEN,BLUE,YELLOW compete among themselves to determine one victorious house. These events are also conducted in school to teach a student importance of teamwork, leadership skills, sportsman spirit, which are going to be helpful for the student in their future life.
