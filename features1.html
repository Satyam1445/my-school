SCHOOL LOCATION
About Location

St. Paul's School - an ICSE medium school is situated in the heart of the city Begusarai. The location of School makes it very much convenient and accessible to all parts of the city. Being situated near NH-31, there is no hindrance in road transport. Students from farther side of the cities are also able to reach conveniently to the school.
